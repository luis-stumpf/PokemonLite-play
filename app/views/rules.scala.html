Welcome to PokemonLite, a fun and interactive game that brings the world of Pokemon to your web browser

Here's how to play:

  • Choose your Pokemon Trainer name and click "Start."
  • Select three Pokemon to form your team. You can choose from a variety of Pokemon, each with its own unique abilities.
  • Battle other trainers, level up your Pokemon, and become the ultimate Pokemon Master!

Game Rules:

Before embarking on your Pokemon adventure, it's essential to understand the game's rules:

  • At the start of each battle, you must choose between two actions: 'Fight' or 'Switch Pokemon.'
  • 'Fight' allows your active Pokemon to use its special move against the opponent.
  • 'Switch Pokemon' enables you to switch your current Pokemon with another from your team.
  • The battle continues until one trainer's team is defeated, or the player's last Pokemon faints.

History of PokemonLite:

PokemonLite is a project born out of a passion for both Pokemon and web development. Developed as an exercise for the Web-Application lecture at HTWG Konstanz, it has grown into a full-fledged Scala Play Project.

Originally created for the Software Engineering course, PokemonLite is now a dynamic online platform where players can engage in epic battles with their favorite Pokemon.

To interact with the code, you can:

  • Compile the code with 'sbt compile.'
  • Run the game using 'sbt run.'
  • Access the Scala 3 REPL with 'sbt console' to experiment and extend the project.
